# Privacy Policy for Help Me!

**Last Updated:** February 2, 2026

## Introduction

Help Me! ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our emergency safety application.

## Privacy-First Design

**We do NOT collect, store, transmit, or share ANY personal data to any servers.**

Help Me! is designed with privacy as the core principle. All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

## What We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ No analytics or usage tracking
- ❌ No personal information sent to servers  
- ❌ No background location tracking
- ❌ No advertising or third-party SDKs
- ❌ No cloud backups or syncing
- ❌ No device identifiers or IP addresses

## Data Stored Locally

The app stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

### Emergency Contacts
- Phone numbers you manually add to the app
- Stored in your device's secure local storage
- Never transmitted to external servers

### Panic History
- Records of emergency button activations
- Timestamps and status of alerts sent
- Stored in your device's secure database (SQLite)
- Used only for your personal reference

### User Preferences
- App settings and configurations
- Stored locally on your device

### Voice Recordings (Optional)
- Audio recordings created using the voice recorder feature
- Stored in your device's local storage
- Never uploaded or transmitted

**Important:** All this data stays on your device. If you uninstall the app, all data is permanently deleted.

## Permissions Explained

The app requires the following Android permissions to function:

### 1. SMS Permission
- **Purpose:** Send emergency text messages to your contacts during SOS activation
- **When Used:** Only when you tap the emergency SOS button
- **Scope:** Messages sent only to contacts you manually configured
- **Note:** Never used for marketing, tracking, or any other purpose

### 2. Phone Call Permission
- **Purpose:** Initiate emergency calls to your first contact
- **When Used:** Only when you tap the emergency SOS button
- **Scope:** Calls made only to the first contact in your list

### 3. Location Permission
- **Purpose:** Capture your GPS coordinates to include in emergency messages
- **When Used:** Only when you tap the emergency SOS button (one-time capture, not continuous tracking)
- **Scope:** Location shared only via SMS to your configured contacts
- **Important:** No background location tracking. Location is captured once during SOS, not continuously.

### 4. Microphone Permission
- **Purpose:** Record audio for Voice Recorder and Safety Cam features
- **When Used:** Only when you manually tap the "Record" button
- **Scope:** Recordings saved locally on your device. Never uploaded.
- **Consent:** Required for recording functionality.

### 5. Camera Permission
- **Purpose:** Record video overlays for Safety Cam feature
- **When Used:** Only when you manually open Safety Cam and tap "Record"
- **Scope:** Videos saved locally on your device.
- **Consent:** required for video functionality.

### 6. Notifications Permission
- **Purpose:** Display background service notification and emergency alerts
- **When Used:** For persistent notification required by Android to keep app responsive
- **Scope:** Local notifications only

**These permissions are used EXCLUSIVELY for user-initiated emergency features and are never used for spying, tracking, or analytics.**

## Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features:

- **Device Encryption:** Android 7+ automatically encrypts all app data
- **Local Storage:** No cloud transmission means no interception risk
- **User Control:** Only you have access to your data

### Recommendations:
- Keep your device locked with a strong password or biometric authentication
- Enable device encryption in Android settings (usually enabled by default)
- Don't share your device with untrusted individuals
- Regular backup using Android's built-in backup (optional)

## Third-Party Services

Help Me! does **NOT** integrate with any:
- Third-party analytics platforms
- Advertising networks
- External servers or cloud services
- Social media integrations

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT** share your data with anyone because we do not collect or access it in the first place.

The only "sharing" that occurs is when **you** trigger an emergency alert or share a file, and the app sends:
- SMS messages to contacts **you** configured
- Your GPS location (captured at SOS moment) to contacts **you** configured
- Emergency call to contact **you** configured
- Audio/Video files (via standard Android share sheet) to apps **you** select
- Audio/Video files (via standard Android share sheet) to apps **you** select

This is direct device-to-device communication controlled entirely by you.

## Children's Privacy

Help Me! is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used this app, please uninstall it from their device.

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect your data, there is no data for us to access, modify, or delete. You have full control:

- **Access:** All your data is visible in the app's settings and history screens
- **Modify:** You can edit emergency contacts anytime in the app
- **Delete:** Uninstall the app to permanently delete all local data
- **Export:** (Future feature) Export your panic history for your records
